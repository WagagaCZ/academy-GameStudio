Mastermind

How it works

  • Your goal is to guess the hidden color code
  • Colors may repeat, or be absent in the code
  • Press "Own" to change the code's length and the number of tries
  • Your guesses appear in the left column and their corrections in the right column
  • In corrections, red means it's the right color in the right place while white means the color is present in the code but the particular place is wrong
  • You must be logged in to see top scores in the table below
